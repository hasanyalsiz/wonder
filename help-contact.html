Delivery

Worldwide Delivery

Wordery offers FREE worldwide delivery on ALL orders.

Most standard UK orders are sent via Royal Mail and selected orders, such as Express Delivery, via partner couriers.

Most international orders are sent via Royal Mail priority air mail.

We will always try to send your order in as few packages as possible. However sometimes we have to send your items separately, but we'll display this clearly in My Wordery orders.
